# Leave Policy

## Annual Leave

All permanent employees are entitled to **15 working days** (3 weeks) of paid annual leave per 12-month cycle, as per the Basic Conditions of Employment Act (BCEA).

- Leave accrues at **1.25 days per month** of service.
- Annual leave must be taken within **6 months** of the end of the annual leave cycle.
- Unused leave may be carried over to the next cycle with manager approval, up to a maximum of **5 days**.
- Leave requests must be submitted at least **5 working days** in advance for planned leave.
- Annual leave may not be taken during the first **3 months** of employment (probation period).

## Sick Leave

Employees are entitled to **30 days** of paid sick leave over a **3-year cycle**, as per the BCEA.

- In the first 6 months of employment, an employee is entitled to **1 day** of paid sick leave for every **26 days** worked.
- A medical certificate is required for absences of **more than 2 consecutive days**.
- A medical certificate is also required if the employee is absent on a **Monday or Friday**, or on a day immediately before or after a public holiday.
- Sick leave that has not been used does not carry over or get paid out.
- Abuse of sick leave may result in disciplinary action.

## Family Responsibility Leave

Permanent employees who have been employed for **longer than 4 months** and who work at least **4 days per week** are entitled to **3 days** of paid family responsibility leave per annual cycle.

- Family responsibility leave may be taken when:
  - The employee's child is born.
  - The employee's child is sick.
  - In the event of the death of the employee's spouse, life partner, parent, adoptive parent, grandparent, child, adopted child, grandchild, or sibling.
- Proof of the event may be required (e.g., birth certificate, death certificate, medical certificate).
- Unused family responsibility leave does not carry over to the next cycle.
